Box Star Unfolding

Dani Demas and Yu Xuan Hong

This visualization shows the star unfolding for a rectangular prism, given a source point on the purple face. Cutting along the black lines and refolding into the prism will identify all eight copies of the source point. The red lines are the edges of the Voronoi diagram of the copies of the source point, leading to the cut locus.

Use the sliders below to change the relative dimensions of the prism, and click and drag the source point to change its position on the purple face.
